That It started during a particularly stressful period in my life. I had a calendar packed with commitments, a home filled with possessions I rarely used, and a constant feeling of being busy yet accomplishing very little. The breaking point came when I realized I owned three separate vegetable peelers but couldn't remember the last time I'd actually enjoyed cooking a meal.That's when I began questioning: How had I accumulated so much—both physically and mentally—that meant so little? The pursuit of simplicity became my path back to what truly matters.
